Our Story

Founded in 2015 by agricultural visionary and a business couple Josephine Njoroge & Beautah Ngugi, LEMAFIX began as a small family operation Thika Kiambu county. Through relentless commitment to quality and innovative feed formulas, we've grown to become one of Kenya's most trusted animal nutrition providers. Today, we supply over nationwide while maintaining our core values of integrity and sustainability.
